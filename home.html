Frequently Asked Questions

What is ChainCare?

ChainCare is a decentralized healthcare platform that empowers patients to control and securely manage their medical records using Web3 technologies.

How is my data secured?

Your medical data is stored using blockchain technology, ensuring that it's encrypted, decentralized, and only accessible by you and authorized healthcare providers.

How do smart contracts work in healthcare?

Smart contracts automate processes like insurance claims and payments by executing predefined rules once certain conditions are met, reducing the need for intermediaries.

Can I revoke access to my medical records?

Yes, with ChainCare, you have complete control over who can access your medical records, and you can revoke access at any time.
